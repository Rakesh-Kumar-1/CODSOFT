JEFF BEZOS

Jeff Bezos is an American entrepreneur and business magnate, best known as the founder of Amazon, one of the world's largest and most influential e-commerce companies. Born on January 12, 1964, in Albuquerque, New Mexico, Bezos founded Amazon in 1994, initially as an online bookstore, before expanding it into a global e-commerce giant. Under his leadership, Amazon diversified into various sectors including cloud computing, artificial intelligence, and media. Bezos also founded Blue Origin, a private aerospace manufacturer and spaceflight services company, with the aim of enabling private human space travel. In 2021, he stepped down as Amazon's CEO to focus on other ventures and philanthropic efforts. Bezos is one of the world's wealthiest individuals and a prominent figure in technology and business innovation.
